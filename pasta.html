Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Heat olive oil and add tomato sauce.
  3. Mix boiled pasta and stir well.
  4. Top with cheese and serve hot.
