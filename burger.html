Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 chicken or veggie patty
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Lettuce and tomato slices
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp ketchup

Steps

  1. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  2. Cook the patty until golden and crispy.
  3. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on buns.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato slices, patty, and cheese.
  5. Cover with the top bun and serve hot.
