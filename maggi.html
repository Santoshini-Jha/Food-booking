🍝 Maggi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggie noodles
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (optional)
  • Maggie tastemaker masala

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan and add Maggie tastemaker masala.
  2. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for another minute.
  4. Stir well until the water evaporates slightly.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
